Layer Cakes

These dessert cakes are available in 3 sizes: 8" round (10-12 servings), 9" round (16-20 servings), & 1/4 sheet cake (2-layer, 20-24 servings). All cakes may be prepared without nuts, if preferred. A compimentary written icing message and decorative border may be included, if desired.

Banana Nut

If you like Banana Nut Bread, you'll love this adaptation! Flavorful layers with cream cheese frosting between and on top. A classic southern taste!

8" size: $35 | 9" size: $45 | 1/4 sheet cake: $50

Black Forest

Rich chocolate layers filled with chocolate-cherry sauce and frosted with chocolate buttercream. Sweet cherries, chocolate curls, and freshly whipped cream put the finishing touches on top of this scrumptious delight.

8" size: $40 | 9" size: $50 | 1/4 sheet cake: $55

Carrot

Cooked, finely chopped carrots blended inot the cake batter make this cake incredibly moist and delicious. A family favorite with cream cheese frosting and chopped nuts.

8" size: $40 | 9" size: $50 | 1/4 sheet cake: $55

Coconut

Moist coconut is mixed in a yellow cake batter and blended with cream cheese frosting to make layers of light, creamy goodness. Covered in more coconut, this cake gives a lovely white presentation.

8" size: $35 | 9" size: $45 | 1/4 sheet cake: $50

German Chocolate

Cooked coconut pecan filling frosts the outside and between the layers of this sweet chocolate cake. A very popular choice!

8" size: $35 | 9" size: $45 | 1/4 sheet cake: $50

Hummingbird

Chock full of chopped bananas, pineapple, and nuts with a touch of cinnamon. Frosted with cream cheese frosting and nuts. Perfect for brunch or anytime.

8" size: $35 | 9" size: $45 | 1/4 sheet cake: $50

Italian Cream

Yellow cake layers combined with coconut and chopped pecans. Filled and frosted with delicious cream cheese frosting and more pecans. A family favorite.

8" size: $35 | 9" size: $45 | 1/4 sheet cake: $50

Red Velvet

You don't have to wait until Christmas to order this popular cake. We offer it anytime of the year! Cream cheese frosting fills and tops velvety red layers that have a hint of almond flavoring. This cake will melt in your mouth!

8" size: $35 | 9" size: $45 | 1/4 sheet cake: $50

Rich Chocolate

Satisfy your chocolate craving with rich chocolate cake layers filled and frosted with your choice of vanilla or chocolate butter cream and topped with chocolate curls.

8" size: $35 | 9" size: $45 | 1/4 sheet cake: $50

Strawberry

Fresh chopped strawberries are added to yellow layers to give a burst of strawberry flavor in every bite. More strawberries are combined in our cream cheese frosting. A delightful pink color creates a lovely presentation.

8" size: $35 | 9" size: $45 | 1/4 sheet cake: $50

Yellow Cake

Buttery cake layers filled and topped with your choice of vanilla or chocolate butter-cream frosting. A favorite for birthdays or any special occasion.

8" size: $30 | 9" size: $40 | 1/4 sheet cake: $45

White Almond

Almond flavoring is added to white cake layers (vanilla flavoring may be substituted). Filled with your choice of apricot, rasberry, strawberry, or vanilla or chocolate butter-cream between layers and covered with vanilla or almond buttercream frosting. Sliced almonds on the sides (optional) and white chocolate curls on top make this cake a real show stopper!

8" size: $40 | 9" size: $50 | 1/4 sheet cake: $55

Pound Cakes

10" round - "bundt"/tube style (20-24 servings)

Cinnamon Apple Pound

Fresh Granny Smith apples are sliced and coated with cinnamon sugar to make the filling for this buttery vanilla pound cake. Topped with creamy caramel glaze, if desired.

$40

Cream Cheese Pound

Cream cheese added to traditional vanilla (or almond) flavored pound cake batter results in an especially moist and flavorful cake. Sprinkled with powdered sugar.

$30

Chocolate Cream Cheese Pound

Our chream cheese pound recipe with dark cocoa added and topped with rich chocolate glaze.

$35

Lemon Pound

We use freshly squeezed lemons to flavor both cake and glaze topping. Extra luscious when served warm.

$35

Cheesecakes

Chocolate Chip Cheesecake

Vanilla cheesecake in an oreo cookie crust sprinkled with mini chocolate chips throughout. Topped with rich chocolate curls and freshly whipped cream.

$35

Harvest Pumpkin Cheesecake

Smooth pumpkin with cinnamon, nutmeg, and allspice blend with cream cheese in a sweet gingersnap crust. Topped with sugared pecans and whipped cream. Perfect for Thanksgiving!

$55

Marble Cheesecake

Melted chocolate swirled throughout our vanilla cheesecake baked in an oreo cookie crust. Topped with rich chocolate curls and whipped cream.

$35

New York Cheesecake

Creamy vanilla cheesecake in a cinnamon graham cracker crust. Topped with vanilla sour cream glaze, trimmed with real whipped cream.
